[SEC v Coinbase](https://www.jdsupra.com/legalnews/sec-v-coinbase-on-to-the-second-circuit-1310501/)

case arose from the SEC's June 2023 enforcement action, alleging that Coinbase operated as an unregistered national securities exchange, broker and clearing agency by intermediating transactions in 13 digital assets that the SEC claimed were investment contracts and, thus, securities.
